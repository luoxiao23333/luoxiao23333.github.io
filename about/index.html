Brief Bio

Hi! I am Xiao Luo. I am currently a Master’s student of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Georgia Tech. I am now work for CS 8903 Special Problem advised by Prof. Yingyan (Celine) Lin, specifically on Improving the Hardware Efficiency of Transformer Models in Mobile Applications.

I have more than 4 years experience of c++ and Linux, specifically in Unix sockets, Qt5, c++11-17, polymorphism and interfaces, OpenMP(I) for the parallel program, Kubernetes, containerd and cgroupv2 for resource management, web servers, Mysql, etc.

And for Machine Learning, I can use Pytorch proficiently to creats and modify DL models. And I have took Statistical Machine Learning last semester and have basic math foundation of some statistical ML models.

I enjoy designing, deploying, or analysing a realistic system that can empower the future, and enjoy the happiness to build and confine a system like an artist. With years of engineering experience and 4 years learning OS, networking, math, etc. in my undergraduate, I am more and more advent in Machine Learning System and looking for any Ph.D. opportunities in such areas.

Appreciation for all who reach here and get to know me! Hope everyone can enjoy their own happiness every now and then.
